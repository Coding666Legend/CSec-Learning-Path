### ⚫ December 2026 — FULL SIMULATION

**Phase:** Mock Reports · Report Writing Practice · Debrief Prep

|Week|Dates|Focus|
|---|---|---|
|Week 1|Dec 1 – Dec 7|Write a full professional report from your November mock|
|Week 2|Dec 8 – Dec 14|Second mock engagement → second full report|
|Week 3|Dec 15 – Dec 21|Practice debrief out loud · record yourself · time it|
|Week 4|Dec 22 – Dec 31|Rest 🎄 · light review only · no grinding|

**🎯 Month Goal:**

```
[ ] Write 2 complete professional pentest reports

[ ] Practice the 15-min debrief call 4–5 times (record it)
[ ] Rest the last week — you've earned it
```

**💡 Tip:** The report is harder than the exam for most people. Two full practice reports in December means you go into February confident, not panicking.
